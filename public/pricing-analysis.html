Pricing Analysis & Token Valuation

Package pricing based on token economy, infrastructure costs,
creative payouts, and European market rates

February 2026 · Confidential

1Executive Summary

BrandBite operates a token-based creative-as-a-service model where 1 token ≈ 1 hour of European creative work. This document derives package pricing from three inputs: designer payout obligations, platform infrastructure costs, and competitive market positioning.

Key finding: At a blended European creative rate of €28/hr and a 60% base payout, the break-even token price is €46.67. With volume discounts across four tiers, recommended retail prices range from €30–€45 per token, yielding plan prices of €2,250–€15,000/month.

2Token Economy Fundamentals

Every job in BrandBite has a fixed token cost charged to the customer and a payout amount credited to the assigned creative. The ratio follows a 60/40 split at the base tier.

6Token Price Derivation

Token retail price      = Designer rate ÷ Payout %
                        = €28.00 ÷ 0.60

12Key Formulas

-- Token retail price
token_price = designer_hourly_rate ÷ payout_percent
            = €28 ÷ 0.60 = €46.67 (before volume discount)

-- Monthly plan price
plan_monthly = tokens × discounted_per_token_rate

-- Annual plan price
plan_annual = plan_monthly × 12 × (1 − yearly_discount)

-- Designer payout per job
payout = token_cost × quantity × (payout_percent ÷ 100)

-- Net margin per token
margin = token_price − designer − stripe − infra − overhead
